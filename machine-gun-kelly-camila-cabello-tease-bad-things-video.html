Machine Gun Kelly And Camila Cabello Tease Bad Things Video

Ever since Machine Gun Kelly and Fifth Harmony’s Camila Cabello dropped their collaborative single, “Bad Things,” a few weeks back, the duo’s been everywhere in attempt to move it up the charts. From lyric videos to performances on The Tonight Show, it seems to be working: the track is currently number 19 on the pop radio charts and 28 on the Hot 100 (jumping from 46 the week before). So, what’s going to come next? The music video, of course.

Recommended Videos

While tomorrow is the magic day for the video’s release, a couple of teasers have emerged for those who can’t wait. Between emotional scenes featuring the duo, it looks as though the treatment will fall in line with the song’s concept: being in love with someone who you know might not be the best for you, but you just don’t care. The coolest part about the clip is that it looks much more cinematic than your standard music video, so it has the potential to turn into something special.

“Bad Things” isn’t very close to the top just yet, but considering how fast it’s rising by the day, it may turn into an overnight hit with the help of the music video. Both Machine Gun Kelly and Camila Cabello have the personalities for being solo stars, and this song could be their ticket to getting there.
