Gio Benitez ABC News Correspondent Marries Tommy DiDario

Jan 1, 1 Love is in the air! ABC News correspondent Gio Benitez married his boyfriend, Tommy DiDario, in a romantic ceremony in Miami on Saturday, April 17. The couple tied the knot in front of their closest friends, family and Benitez’s fellow ABC News journalists inside of the Walton House, a historic English-style cottage in Florida, according to The New York Times. DiDario’s sister, Megs A. DiDario, officiated the event, for which she became a Universal Life minister.
