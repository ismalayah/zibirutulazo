Birmingham sports radio station WJOX announced Tuesday it will soon air a new talk show featuring former Auburn football star Cole Cubelic, Alabama beat writer Aaron Suttles, and former WJOX radio personality Landrum Roberts.

After graduating from Homewood High School, Cubelic played center at Auburn from 1996-2000, serving as a team captain during his senior year. In addition to hosting the Cube Show on WUMP, he also works as a college football analyst for ESPN and the SEC Network. Suttles has been the Alabama beat writer for the Tuscaloosa News since 2006, and Roberts was a show host from 2001-04 for WJOX before working in the construction industry for the last 14 years,.

The show starts March 26, and has not been named yet, but will replace the spot formerly held by The Roundtable, a show featuring Lance Taylor, Ryan Brown and Jim Dunnaway. The Roundtable will now be the WJOX morning show, replacing The Opening Drive, which was canceled at the end of February.
