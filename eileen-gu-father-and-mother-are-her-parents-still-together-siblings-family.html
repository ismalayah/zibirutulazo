Eileen Gu Father And Mother: Are Her Parents Still Together? Siblings &amp; Family

The freestyle Skier Eileen Gu was born to an American father and Chinese mother. Are her parents still together? Let us learn more about her siblings and family details below!

Eileen Gu is an American-born skier.

Her original name is Eileen Feng Gu but she is also known as Ailing Gu. She has been competing for China 2019 and has also won two gold medals at the 2020 event.

In addition to that, she landed gold across two events – superpipe and slopestyle. Further, she also won bronze in the Big Air competition.

As a matter of fact, she was the first Chinese athlete to win gold, and the first rookie to walk away with three medals.

Eileen Gu Father And Mother: Are Her Parents Still Together?

She developed her passion for the sport after she moved to the San Francisco Bay Area while she earned an MBA from Stanford University. On the other hand, not much has been disclosed to the media about her father, not even his name.

As there is no dispute reported as of now, it is speculated that the athlete’s parents are still together. Further, it is known that the athlete’s mother put young Eileen in Ski lessons at Lake Tahoe in order to keep her daughter with her.
